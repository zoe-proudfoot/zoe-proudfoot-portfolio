About Me

I am a web developer hailing from the picturesque locale of Gisborne, New Zealand. Driven by a desire for career metamorphosis, I embarked on a transformative journey in June 2023 by enrolling in Dev Academy Aotearoa. This monumental decision thrust me into the captivating realm of software development, a world where creativity and innovation converge.

As I delved deeper into the multifaceted world of software development, a profound affection blossomed within me for the aesthetic aspects of web development. The artistry of CSS and the foundational structure of HTML became my muse, setting the stage for a passionate pursuit of web design.

Throughout the Bootcamp phases of my Dev Academy Aotearoa experience, my horizons broadened significantly. I was exposed to a plethora of technologies and methodologies that form the bedrock of modern web development. The curriculum was meticulously crafted to equip me with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in this dynamic field. Key areas of focus included databases with SQL, the intricacies of APIs, and mastering the art of crafting responsive and dynamic web applications using React, powered by the versatile JavaScript and Typescript languages.

In conclusion, my journey into the realm of software development through Dev Academy Aotearoa has not only ignited a fervent passion for web design but has also equipped me with a comprehensive skill set to thrive in this ever-evolving domain.
